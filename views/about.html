Leech

Leech is a serverless, databaseless link shortener.

This server (the one running this website) doesn't actually do much of anything. It just shortens your link and then stores it in S3.

The actually link redirection (i.e. going from http://lee.ch/b26d8sj3 to http://espn.go.com/, actually takes place on a static website which is all stored on S3.

Each time someone clicks on your shortened link, they actually open a page. That page is configured with Google Analytics.

Any and all click data makes its way back to Google Analytics, which acts as your database. Best of all, it gives you free access to Google Analytics reports, metrics, and demographics.
